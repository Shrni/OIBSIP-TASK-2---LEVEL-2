Mukesh Ambani Tribute Page

Remembering the life and achievements of India's richest man

Early Life and Education

Mukesh Ambani was born on April 19, 1957, in Aden, Yemen. His father Dhirubhai Ambani was a well-known Indian entrepreneur and founder of Reliance Industries. Mukesh Ambani completed his schooling from Hill Grange High School in Mumbai and later pursued chemical engineering from the Institute of Chemical Technology.

Business Career

Mukesh Ambani started his business career by helping his father in managing their textile company. He later played a key role in the development of Reliance Industries, which grew to become one of India's largest conglomerates. In 2002, he launched Reliance Infocomm, which later became Reliance Communications, and revolutionized the Indian telecom industry. He also launched Reliance Jio in 2016, which became India's largest mobile network operator in just three years.

Personal Life

Mukesh Ambani is married to Nita Ambani and they have three children together. He is known for his love for cricket and owns the Indian Premier League team Mumbai Indians. He is also a philanthropist and has donated millions of dollars to various charitable causes.

Achievements and Awards

Mukesh Ambani has received numerous awards and recognition for his contributions to the business world. He was ranked as the 10th richest person in the world by Forbes in 2021, with a net worth of $84.5 billion. He has also been honored with the Padma Vibhushan, India's second-highest civilian award, for his services in trade and industry.
